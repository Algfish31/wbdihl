About the List

What is WBDL?

The Web Browser Demon List (WBDL) is a ranking of the hardest levels playable on the web-based version of Geometry Dash. The goal is simply to have fun, you shouldn't see this list as an alternative to buying the game!

The Ranking System

Levels are ranked based on their estimated difficulty. We review every placement and ensure that the list is accurate, even if that may take some adjustments along the way.

Beating levels will award you with List Points, these are calculated using a similar system as the Pointercrate Demonlist. The harder the level, the more points. Try to reach the top of the Leaderboard!

About the game

WBDL utilizes a modded version of the GeometryDash.com demo. We are not affiliated with RobTop Games in any way.

Our version introduces the ability to load custom levels via ID as well as a few extra objects and QOL features, such as StartPos switcher.
